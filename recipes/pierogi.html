Pierogi z Kapusta i Grzybami


Description

Pierogi, an Eastern European (or more directly), a Polish delight are a wonderful yet simple dish. A staple of Polish households across the world, the humble pierogi uses simple to find ingredients and transforms them into a wonderful, golden brown dumpling that makes every bite to die for. Pierogi can be made with many types of fillings, but in this recipe we will making a childhood favourite of mine; kapusta z grzybami, or in English; Sauerkraut and mushrooms. I personally believe you can never go wrong with a pierogi. Never.
