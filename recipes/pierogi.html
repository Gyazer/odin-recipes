Pierogi z Kapusta i Grzybami


Description

Pierogi, an Eastern European (or more directly), a Polish delight are a wonderful yet simple dish.
A staple of Polish households across the world, the humble pierogi uses simple to find ingredients and transforms them into a wonderful, golden brown dumpling that makes every bite to die for.
Pierogi can be made with many types of fillings, but in this recipe we will making a childhood favourite of mine; kapusta z grzybami, or in English; Sauerkraut and mushrooms.
I personally believe you can never go wrong with a pierogi. Never.


Ingredients

For the filling

  • 2tbsp vegetable oil
  • 15g butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 250g mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 300g (drained weight) sauerkraut (half a 900g jar)

For the pierogi dough

  • 360g plain flour
  • 240ml hot (not boiling) water
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4tsp salt
  • 1tsp vegetable oil

For the topping

This is optional, but makes the pierogi 10x better!

  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 20g butter
